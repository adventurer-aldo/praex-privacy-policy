Privacy Policy for PraEx (Praticar Exame de Condução)

Last updated: July 8, 2026

AdventurerAldo ("I", "me", or "my"), an individual developer, developed the PraEx (Praticar Exame de Condução) mobile application (the "App"). This page explains what information is collected, how it's used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address. However, the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, and AdMob collects certain data automatically in order to serve ads. This data includes:

  • Advertising ID (a device identifier used for ad personalization and frequency capping)
  • App activity data, such as ad interactions (impressions and clicks), used for measurement and performance reporting

How This Information Is Used

The data described above is used solely to serve and measure advertisements within the App, including showing you ads that may be more relevant to you and preventing the same ad from being shown too frequently.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. AdMob may collect and process data as described above on my behalf. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from partner sites/apps

Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal data. Data collected via AdMob is shared with Google as described above for the sole purpose of ad delivery and measurement.

Data Retention and Deletion

I do not directly store any personal data on my own servers. Data collected by AdMob is retained and managed according to Google's own data retention policies. You can manage your Advertising ID and ad personalization settings at any time through your device's system settings (Settings > Privacy > Ads on most Android devices).

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 13 and over. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through this App, please contact me so I can address it.

Data Security

All data collected through the App via AdMob is transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time via your device's ad settings. This will not stop ads from appearing, but ads will no longer be based on your Advertising ID.
